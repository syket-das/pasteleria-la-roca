Our Story

MADE WITH LOVE,
BAKED WITH TRADITION

Pasteleria La Roca is a family-owned Mexican bakery in the heart of West Dallas, bringing authentic flavors and stunning custom cakes to the community. What started with a passion for baking has grown into one of Dallas' most recommended bakeries — with a 100% recommendation rate from 92 Facebook reviews.

"Custom cakes for any occasion and the best Mexican bread — that's our promise to every customer who walks through our doors."

From perfectly golden conchas and sweet semita de aniz to breathtaking quinceañera cakes and three-tier wedding creations — every item is handmade fresh. Our customers aren't just buying pastries, they're celebrating life's biggest moments with us.
